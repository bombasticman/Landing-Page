What are battle types?

Characters in fighting games tend to fall under different playstyles/archetypes to fully utulize their moveset to their best potential. Thus, these are categorizations officially designated by Arc System Works to help players new to the series to get an idea of what archetype Strive's playable characters fall into.

Definition of each battle type

Balance

Their moves are easy to use. If you're debating who to play, give them a try!

High Speed

They can move about at incredible speed. Take advantage of their movement to take control of neutral.

Long Range

Their long range makes them good at fighting from a distance. Use their strong pokes to handle whatever your opponent throws at you.

One Shot

Each of their moves are strong, so carefully waiting for a gap in the opponent's offense creates an opportunity to land a fatal blow.

Power

Crush your opponent before they have the chance to fight back with his unrivaled power!

Power Throw

Nothing feels better than landing one of their damaging throws and changing the tide of battle, even if it takes work to get there.

Rush

Overwhelm the opponent with fast-paced, continuous offense at close range!

Shooting

They can throw out powerful projectiles. Their strong pressure keeps the opponent locked down.

Technical

It takes some serious training to play them well, but they have amazing potential.

Unique

They may take some getting used to, but in the proper hands they can launch an unparalleled offense.
